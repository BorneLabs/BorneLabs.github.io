What is BorneLabs, and what does it do?

BorneLabs is a community-driven hub for technological and creative innovation. Our mission is to inspire and empower developers, innovators, and enthusiasts by providing tools, skills, resources, and opportunities for collaboration.

How can I join a community at BorneLabs?

You can join by signing up through our platform and selecting the community that aligns with your interests or expertise.

What industries does BorneLabs focus on for AI applications?

We cater to industries like healthcare, robotics, gaming, and sustainability, among others.

Do I need prior experience to join a community?

No, we welcome enthusiasts at all skill levels and provide resources to help you grow.

Our Top Open Source Projects 

Grid Forum (SAS.1)

Grid Forum is an open-source platform where AI models collaborate and share knowledge, enhancing their collective intelligence. It also includes features like Glink, a secure browser, for privacy and efficient AI development.

KU Students App (SAS.2)

The KU Students App combines the Student Portal and KUSOMA, streamlining tasks like registration and accessing resources. Its user-friendly design enhances academic and administrative efficiency for Kenyatta University students.

Learn More
Community Page (SAS.3)

The Community Page is platform designed to foster collaboration, idea-sharing, and project development. It is hub where individuals connect with like-minded peers and build communities of contributors who support and enhance their projects.

Learn More
Cognito App (SAS.4)

Cognito is a matrix-themed, anonymous chat forum that champions free speech. Blending real-time chat with structured discussions, it offers a secure, censorship-free space for open conversations.

Learn More
Tab Killer (SAS.5)

Tab Killer is a ruthless yet hilarious Chrome extension that lets you close tabs with a single click while tracking your "kills." Perfect for chaotic minimalists, digital hitmen, or anyone drowning in open tabs but only using a few.

Learn More
Flux Pulse Motor (IAE.1)

The IAE-1 Flux Pulse Motor is an experimental propulsion system designed to enhance thrust efficiency through controlled flux pulses. By optimizing energy conversion and minimizing losses, it aims to create a more efficient and scalable propulsion technology for future applications.
