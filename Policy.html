Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BorneLabs. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services, website, and community platforms. By accessing or using any of our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to improve our services and provide a better experience for our community members. Personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details may be collected when you register, subscribe, or interact with BorneLabs. Additionally, we gather usage data, including IP addresses, browser types, and patterns of activity on our website. Cookies and tracking technologies are used to enhance user experience, analyze trends, and improve our services. Any user-generated content, such as forum posts, comments, or shared resources, is also collected and stored within our community platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services and products. It facilitates collaboration and communication within our community and enables us to send updates, newsletters, and important notifications related to BorneLabs. We also use this data to enhance security, detect fraud, and ensure the integrity of our platform. Compliance with legal obligations and enforcement of our terms of service are additional purposes for data usage.

4. How We Share Your Information

BorneLabs does not sell or rent personal data. However, we may share information with third-party vendors who assist in operating our website and services. If you participate in forums, discussions, or collaborations, any content you share may be visible to other users within the community. In cases where legal obligations require, we may disclose information to comply with legal processes or to protect the rights and safety of BorneLabs and its users.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. While we take reasonable steps to ensure security, no online platform can be entirely secure. We encourage users to exercise caution when sharing sensitive information online.

6. Protection of Intellectual Property & Project Submissions

BorneLabs is committed to fostering innovation while ensuring fair treatment of contributors. If you engage with BorneLabs in an open-source capacity, your contributions will be considered open-source and made freely available to the community. For revenue-sharing agreements, ownership and financial terms will be outlined in a separate written agreement. In cases of expedited project completion, payment terms and deliverables will be clearly defined before development begins. BorneLabs is not obligated to pursue, develop, or compensate for unsolicited project ideas or proposals submitted without a prior agreement. While we respect intellectual property rights, we reserve the right to use our expertise, resources, and knowledge base in ways that may align with submitted ideas without claiming ownership over externally developed intellectual property unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal information. Additionally, they may opt out of marketing communications and restrict the processing of their data under certain circumstances. Any requests regarding data privacy should be directed to our designated contact email.

8. Third-Party Links

Our services may contain links to third-party websites. BorneLabs is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites, and users should review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit.

9. Children's Privacy

BorneLabs does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take prompt steps to delete it and ensure compliance with applicable regulations.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal obligations. Any modifications will be posted on this page, and users are encouraged to review this policy regularly to stay informed.

For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official communication channels.
